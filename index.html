About Me

And I am a 21-year-old self-taught web developer from Ireland. Creating websites and web applications has always fascinated me, that's why I decided to turn this interest into a career. Despite acquiring considerable knowledge in a relatively short time, I do not rest on my laurels and constantly try to develop, which allows me to write better and better code and create more complex projects. I am a huge fan of BEM methodology, semantics and flat specificity, and in the design of websites I prefer minimalism and simplicity from fireworks and unnecessary animations.
